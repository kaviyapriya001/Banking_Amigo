Now that you have your bank account all set up, you're ready to start using it! You can save your money in your account by depositing some of your money into your account. Or, you can withdraw money from your account to spend on the things you like!

Let's go over some handy terms:

Deposit

Putting money IN your bank accounts. The opposite of withdraw. You can tell your bank teller 'Hi, I would like to deposit some cash.

Withdraw

Taking money (cash) OUT of your bank account. The opposite of deposit. You can tell your bank teller 'Hi, I would like to withdraw some money.'

Automated Teller Machine (ATM)

A machine that allows you to deposit and withdraw money from your accounts. There is a limit on how much you can take out. Don't go over this limit!

Transfer

Transferring money between your accounts. You can tell your bank teller 'Hi, I would like to transfer some money from my chequing to my savings.'
