Finance Tips & Tricks

Tips for choosing a bank

  • It has lots of locations close to where you live and/or work
  • It offers accounts (chequing, savings) that are free for teens and students, no annual fee
  • It has a great reputation and great customer service
  • Ask your parents, guardians, family and friends, or people you trust for which bank they are with and why. Perhaps they have some helpful advice to share!

Tips for choosing a chequing and savings account

  • It should be free for students and teens and does not require you to have a minimum balance
  • It should have a good number of monthly transaction limit
  • If it's a savings account, it should have a good savings interest rate, higher the better

Tips for keeping your debit card safe

Tips for saving and spending your money

  • Be aware of your account balance so you don't spend more than you have!
  • Start saving now and make it into a habit: automatic transfer some money from your chequing account into your savings every month and let your money grow with the savings interest rate
  • Use mobile app to check your balance anytime and anywhere
  • Check your accounts often to make sure no one besides you are using your card
  • Don't be afraid to get help from the bank teller and the bank help line for things you don't know!
